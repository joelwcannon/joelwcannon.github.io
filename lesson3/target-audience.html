Target Audience

Persona A.

20 year old female college student. Budget traveler, with friend. Uses smart phone extensively. Rarely prints. Spontaneous and adventurous.

Persona B.

60 year old semi retired, college educated, married male. Has money to travel comfortably with wife, middle class. Uses desktop to plan trip, phone while touring. Prints documents for reference. Well organized and cautious.


Scenarios

  • What is the weather forecast along my travel route today?
  • What clothing should I wear today?
  • What is the weather forecast along tomorrow's travel route?
  • What clothing do I need to pack?
  • Are there weather alerts for during my trip?
  • What is the weather like during the times I may travel to various destinations?
  • How predictable is the weather during my trip?
  • Notify me of any weather alerts.
